Mpho Gift Mabokela

The gift to the world

Born and Raised in Tembisa, Johannesburg, Mpho has found himself settling in the coastal parts of Cape Town in persuid his career. His curiousity for Technology grew more and more as he was born in a household shot of Technical devices such as television and computers which were then the best Technology inventions. Mpho said himself that "yes, my insecurities of no having are the reasons why I somehow grew interest to be a part of that which I cannot access". Since then, Mpho was was featured in a couple of national TV commercials was able to give his family their first Televison.

After Matriculating from Tembisa High School and lack of finaces to further his studies, Mpho volunteered Makukhanye Alcohol and Drug center where he raised awareness on substance Abuse in his community. It was months later that he got a fulltime position at Marlin Credit where he would be exposed to coporate and profession as he worked as a Customer Care consultant. His interest in the then rising Digital Marketing and Advertising technology emerged. "I remember with my first paycheck I knew I had to get a laptop, even if at home I was mocked to not have my priorities straight as ther were other things that were needed in the house to carry our day to day live, I knew it would be the best investment to make." he said. His decision has proved to be fruitful as it has led to Musical production and Software Development.

Code

"WE ARE CHANDING THE WORLD THROUGH TECHNOLOGY"

Mpho's encounter with Technology would be the start of his Software Development career.He shared that "I remember the first Site I finished. I used Wix platforms but man did I feel smart! Wix exposed me to Search Engines, SEO's exposed me to metadata and I have never stopped wanting to know more about how everything on the internet fetches and provides information to users". Mpho joined WeThinkCode_ where he would further his Software engineering career and has since then focused on growing his knowledge. "At first, when I saw Python in my Bootcamp, I knew that constructing a program will not be as easy as it was when using Wix and Visual Basic, but I was very muich aware that it is doable."

WeThinkCode_ being a work similation enviroment. Mpho has admitted to the difficulties of the program but also thankful for the skills that came with."Now I write programs using fancy frameworks, API's, Http requests, Dart using diffrent languages, I even built a site for a client that is providing a Serve whom also always had a dream of owning a gym program and I am super honored to contribute to making his dreams become reality"
